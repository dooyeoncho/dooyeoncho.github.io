NEW KOREA EPU INDEX

Dooyeon Cho and Husang Kim construct a new Korea economic policy uncertainty index based on major Korean newspapers. In addition to the new overall EPU index, monthly category-specific policy uncertainty indices from January 1990 are available in the following four categories: i) Monetary policy, ii) Fiscal policy, iii) Trade policy, and iv) Foreign exchange rate policy. For more details, please refer to Cho and Kim (2022), "Macroeconomic Effects of Uncertainty Shocks: Evidence from Korea," Journal of Asian Economics, forthcoming.
